Why Choose MediTrack?

Designed for modern hospitals to monitor, triage and treat patients with precision and speed.

Real-Time Patient Monitoring

Continuous streaming of vital signs from bedside devices and wearable IoT sensors into a unified dashboard.

AI-Based Suffering Score System

Intelligent scoring engine that highlights patients in distress based on vitals, pain markers and clinical rules.

Doctor Alert Notification

Multi-channel notifications to on-duty doctors when critical thresholds are breached or trends are detected.

IoT Sensor Integration

Secure integration with bedside monitors, infusion pumps and wearables via standardized healthcare protocols.

Emergency Help Button

One-tap assistance triggers immediate attention for patients experiencing sudden discomfort or pain.

Hospital Crowd Monitoring

Real-time view of occupancy, queue lengths and waiting times across emergency, OPD and in-patient areas.
